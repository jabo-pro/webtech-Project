Ubwoko bw' icyayi

Hari imbaga zitandukanye z'ubuvu buri mu gihugu cya none. Ibuvu bw'ubuvu burira mu buryo bw'umwihariko bw'Asiya, ariko hari naho hateganyijwe kubona ibuvu bwacu mu mirima y'imbuto muri 25 z'ibihugu. Ibuvu bwo mu mahoro cyane burahinduka mu buryo bw'umwihariko bwiza bwo mu bitugu by'ikibanza, n'ibiryo bigurishwa n'abantu kuva ku buryo bw'umwihariko.
